aiXoStats helps you manage your statistics services tracking codes.

== Description ==

* Insert easily your Google Analytics, Woopra, Reinvigorate, Sitemeter or Clicky tracking codes
* Enable name tagging for Woopra or Reinvigorate
* Ban your own IP adress from tracking
* Ban logged administrators from tracking
* Langages supported: English, French


== Installation ==

1. Upload folder aixostats/ to /wp-content/plugins/
2. Activate the plugin in the "extensions" menu
3. Configure in Settings->aiXoStats
